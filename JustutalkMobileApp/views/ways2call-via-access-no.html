• Calling via access number
    • 1. You just dial the Access number 02035146800 or 02037347133 -wait and listen the voice prompt.
    • 2. Then Dial your Destination Number followed by 00 + Country code + Telephone Number + # (or for more minutes without 00, check call rates).
    • 3. Wala! you are connected.

    To make a redial press *2, follow-up call press ## on your phone keypad then repeat the step 2.
